Business Loans — How They Work

What It Is

Working capital and term loans for your commercial tenants who need funding for buildout, equipment, inventory, or daily operations.

How It Protects You

A funded tenant is a tenant who can pay rent, complete their buildout, and succeed in your space. The #1 reason commercial tenants default is cash flow problems.

What You Earn

$200–5,000 per funded deal depending on the loan size and type.

SBA Loan Referrals — How They Work

What It Is

SBA loans are government-backed loans with the lowest interest rates and longest terms. Ideal for established businesses needing significant capital.

How It Protects You

SBA-funded tenants have long-term, affordable financing making them incredibly stable, long-term tenants.

What You Earn

$2,500–5,000 per closed SBA loan. Large commissions because SBA loans are typically $100K–$5M.

Merchant Cash Advance — How It Works

What It Is

An advance on future credit card sales. The business receives a lump sum and repays as a small percentage of daily sales.

How It Protects You

MCAs provide fast capital for retail and restaurant tenants who need bridge funding. A tenant with working capital is a tenant who can pay rent.

What You Earn

$2,000–4,500 per funded MCA. Among the highest-commission referrals in the lending space.

Equipment Financing — How It Works

What It Is

Equipment financing allows commercial tenants to purchase or lease equipment needed for their business without large upfront costs. This includes restaurant equipment, medical devices, HVAC systems, and more.

How It Protects You

Tenants with properly equipped spaces are more productive and profitable, leading to longer lease terms and more reliable rent payments. Equipment financing also often results in property improvements that add value to your space.

What You Earn

$200–2,000 per financed deal depending on equipment value and financing terms. Recurring opportunity as tenants upgrade or expand.

Commercial Mortgage Referrals — How They Work

What It Is

When your tenants are ready to purchase their own commercial space, you can refer them to trusted mortgage lenders who specialize in commercial real estate financing.

How It Protects You

Even when a tenant moves on to their own space, you maintain a positive relationship and earn a referral commission. Many tenants who purchase also keep leasing additional space for growth.

What You Earn

$1,000–5,000 per closed mortgage. Commercial mortgages are large transactions with significant referral commissions.

Deposit Bonds — How They Work

What It Is

A deposit bond replaces the traditional upfront security deposit with a small monthly payment. A surety company guarantees the full deposit amount to you.

How It Protects You

You are covered for the same amount as a traditional deposit. If the tenant causes damage, the surety company pays you up to the bonded amount.

Note for Commercial

Commercial leases typically have larger deposits. Deposit bonds are less common in commercial but can reduce barriers for smaller tenants or startups entering your space.

Rent Flexibility — How It Works

What It Is

Flex lets tenants split their monthly rent into 2 payments. Flex pays you the full rent on the 1st — the tenant repays Flex in two installments.

How It Protects You

You get paid in full, on time, on the 1st. Flex takes on the risk. Useful for smaller commercial tenants with seasonal cash flow.

Small Claims Checklist — Utah

✅ Claims must be $11,000 or less (Utah Code § 78A-8-101)
✅ File in the Justice Court of the county where defendant lives or where contract was made
✅ Filing fee: ~$60-185 depending on claim amount
✅ You must attempt to serve defendant (personal service or certified mail)
✅ Bring all documentation: lease, receipts, photos, payment records
✅ Hearing typically scheduled 14-30 days after filing
✅ Judgment can be collected via wage garnishment or bank levy

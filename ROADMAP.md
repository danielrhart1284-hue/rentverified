# RentVerified Revenue Roadmap

## Layer 1 — Rent Collection & Payment Processing
- **Credit/debit card processing** — 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (landlord or tenant absorbs)
- **ACH, Cash App, Zelle, Venmo** — zero-fee payment methods for tenants

## Layer 2 — Tenant Screening & Applications
- **Application fees** — $35–50 per application (passed to tenant)
- **Background/credit checks** — bundled into screening fee

## Layer 3 — Insurance & Deposit Products
- **Deposit bonds (Jetty / Obligo)** — ~15% commission on every monthly premium
- **Renters insurance (Jetty / Sure)** — $2–5/month recurring per policy
- **Rent guarantee insurance** — $150–200 per policy (15% of annual premium)
- **Lease guarantee (LeaseLock)** — higher commission structure per unit

## Layer 4 — Tenant Financial Tools
- **Credit builder (Boom)** — $1–2/month recurring per tenant enrolled
- **Rent flexibility (Flex)** — tenant pays $14.99/month; referral revenue to be negotiated
- **Utility setup concierge** — $75 per tenant referral
- **Internet/cable setup (AT&T, Xfinity)** — $50–150 per installation

## Layer 5 — Platform Subscriptions & SaaS
- **Landlord subscription tiers** — Free / Pro / Enterprise
- **Agent portal access** — per-seat pricing
- **Commercial portal** — premium tier for commercial property managers

## Layer 6 — Business Funding & Financial Services
- **Business loan referrals** — $200–5,000 per funded deal (GoKapital, Clarify Capital, National Funding)
- **SBA loan referrals** — $2,500–5,000 per closed SBA loan
- **Merchant cash advance** — $2,000–4,500 per funded MCA (Kapitus, Credibly)
- **Equipment financing** — $500–2,000 per funded lease
- **Personal loan leads (tenants)** — $50–300 per qualified lead (Round Sky, Lead Stack Media)
- **Security deposit loan referrals** — $25–100 per funded deposit loan

📄 Property Management Agreement Template

PROPERTY MANAGEMENT AGREEMENT

[Your Business Name] · 3120 Life Platform

PARTIES: This Property Management Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between _______________________ ("Owner") and [Your Business Name] / Daniel Hart, licensed Utah real estate broker ("Manager").
1. PROPERTY: Owner hereby appoints Manager as exclusive property manager for the property located at: _______________________
2. TERM: This Agreement shall commence on _______ and continue for one year, renewing automatically unless either party provides 30 days written notice of termination.
3. MANAGER'S AUTHORITY: Owner grants Manager authority to: (a) advertise the property; (b) screen and select tenants; (c) execute leases on Owner's behalf; (d) collect rents and security deposits; (e) authorize repairs up to $500 without prior approval; (f) initiate eviction proceedings if necessary.
4. MANAGEMENT FEE: Owner agrees to pay Manager a monthly management fee of ___% of collected rent (typically 8–12%) or $___/month flat fee.
5. LEASING FEE: Owner agrees to pay a one-time leasing fee of one month's rent for each new tenancy secured by Manager.
6. TRUST ACCOUNT: Manager shall maintain all tenant funds in a separate trust account as required by Utah Code § 61-2f-301.
7. OWNER REPRESENTATIONS: Owner represents that they hold clear title to the property, that it is in compliance with applicable housing codes, and that they have authority to enter this Agreement.
8. INSURANCE: Owner shall maintain property and liability insurance. Manager maintains E&O and general liability insurance.
Owner Signature / Date
Manager Signature / Date

This template is provided for reference. Consult a Utah real estate attorney before using in a transaction.

Common Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? ▼
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, you're charged the prorated difference immediately. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a contract or commitment? ▼
No contracts, no commitments. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime from your dashboard — no questions asked.
How does the free Starter plan work? ▼
The Starter plan is free forever with up to 3 properties. No credit card required. When you're ready to scale, upgrade to Pro in one click.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. ACH bank transfers are available for Enterprise plans. All payments are encrypted and PCI compliant.
How is RentVerified different from AppFolio or Buildium? ▼
AppFolio starts at $250/mo with a minimum unit requirement. Buildium starts at $55/mo but charges per unit. RentVerified gives you a fully automated leasing pipeline, AI tools, and a complete business OS for a flat $79/mo — no per-unit fees, no minimums.
Do tenants pay anything to use RentVerified? ▼
No. The tenant portal, rent payments, maintenance requests, and lease signing are all free for tenants. The only optional tenant fee is the TransUnion background check (~$40-45), which tenants pay directly to TransUnion.

Financial Resources for Tenants

Need help with your move-in costs or a tough month? We connect you with resources — no judgment.

🛡️

Security Deposit Assistance

Can't afford the full deposit? See if you qualify for a deposit alternative or assistance.

▼
Deposit Bonds: Ask your landlord if they accept deposit bonds — you'd pay ~$17/month instead of the full deposit upfront. Check with your property manager.

Utah Emergency Deposit Programs:
- Salt Lake County: Community Action Services, (801) 359-2444
- Utah County: Community Action Services, (801) 373-8200
- Davis County: Davis Community Housing Authority, (801) 482-6900
- Weber County: Ogden Housing Authority, (801) 627-7272

Emergency Rent Help

Behind on rent? Find emergency assistance programs and short-term options.

▼
Utah Assistance Programs by County:
- Salt Lake County: Community Action, (801) 359-2444
- Utah County: Community Action Provo, (801) 373-8200
- Davis County: Davis Community Housing Authority
- Weber County: Ogden Housing Authority

Flex Rent Splitting: If your landlord accepts Flex, you can split rent into 2 payments per month for $14.99/mo.

211 Utah Helpline: Dial 211 or visit 211utah.org for comprehensive help.

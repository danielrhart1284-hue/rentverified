Why This Matters Legally
If a contractor causes injury or property damage on your rental while operating with an expired license or lapsed insurance, you as the property owner may share liability. Utah courts have held landlords responsible when they hired contractors they "knew or should have known" were unqualified. 3120 Life's compliance tracking gives you documented proof you verified credentials before every job.
